Dedication

I value dedication in myself and the people I have around me. Having dedication, including loyalty, in a team is crucial to a teams success and well being.

Trust

I value trust among the people I have around me. Having trust with your teammates and friends, I believe, is very important. Knowing you can trust the people around you will not only help you succeed but them as well.

Organization

I value organization in both personal and professional life. Having organizational skills is so important to stay on top of your work which will lead to success and hopefully happiness.

Adaptability

I value adaptability in myself and people I work with. Having the ability to adjust to bad situations or adjust quickly and effectively is very important. Having the ability when bad things happen, they can overcome that.

Unconditional Love

I value unconditional love more in my persoanl life than proffetional but it still has an affect in the workplace. The idea of a person's value not being tied to the work or perfection is very important to the workspace.
